Our Story

With years of experience in the industry, our team specializes in creating custom wig solutions and stunning makeup looks for all occasions.

Our Services

Personalized Wig Styling

Custom wig cuts, styles, and treatments tailored to your face shape and personal preferences.

Price Ranges by Inches:

  • 12-14 inches: $80 - $120
  • 16-18 inches: $100 - $150
  • 20-22 inches: $120 - $180
  • 24+ inches: $150 - $220

Coloring & Maintenance

Professional coloring, bleaching, revamping, and maintenance services to keep your wigs looking flawless.

Services Include:

  • Custom coloring and bleaching
  • Wig repair and restoration
  • Deep conditioning treatments
  • Styling product application

Frontal & Closure Installation

Expert installation of frontal and closure pieces for a natural, seamless look.

Installation Options:

  • 360 Frontal Installation: $150 - $250
  • Full Frontal Installation: $120 - $200
  • Closure Installation: $80 - $150

Hair Treatments & Revamping

Specialized treatments to restore and revitalize your wigs and natural hair.

Treatment Options:

  • Protein treatments
  • Moisture restoration
  • Color correction
  • Custom repair services

Standard Makeup

Perfect for everyday wear or special occasions, our standard makeup service enhances your natural features.

Price: $60 - $80

Includes consultation, application, and light touch-ups.

Evening & Photoshoot Makeup

Dramatic, long-lasting makeup designed for evening events or professional photography.

Price: $90 - $120

Includes false lashes, detailed contouring, and professional setting.

Bridal Makeup

Our signature bridal service ensures you look radiant on your special day with makeup that lasts from ceremony to reception.

Price: $150 - $250

Includes trial session, day-of application, and touch-up kit.

Bridal Party Packages

Special rates for bridal parties to ensure everyone looks their best on your big day.

Packages Start at: $100/person

Group discounts available for parties of 3 or more.

Important Notes:

  • Travel fees may apply for services outside our studio location
  • Bridal services require a 50% deposit to secure your date
  • All prices are subject to change based on specific requirements
